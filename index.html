We believe that mediums such as arts, crafts and movement are powerful tools to direct our attention to the present moment and promote a happy state, where learning takes place through exploration and fun.

This is the guiding principle at the heart of our programs. 

Together with our participants, we aim to create a unique multi-sensory learning experience that sparks their imagination, sense of creativity, and supports and empowers them in their unique developmental journey.

Sigalit Somech

Director and
facilitator

My name is Sigalit (Sigal) and I have been living in Melbourne for the last 16 years.

I am a mother and a qualified Early childhood educator with 13 years education experience.

I teach Hebrew for primary age children, and I am also a qualified children’s Yoga teacher.

I love working with children and I provide an enrichment program for children with additional needs which supports their skill development in between their therapy sessions. I create personalised activities based on the child's needs and interests and feel so happy to see them thrive.

Children with self-managed NDIS funds can use our service under the NDIS.

I started the multi-sensory approach to Hebrew program with the vision to create a space for the Hebrew speaking children to engage and interact, and sharpen their Hebrew speaking skills in interactive and non-formal way.

I started Art Works Magic together with my husband with the desire to bring happiness to children and families in the community.

Our workshops keep expending by demand and now also deliver a range of different programs, like mother group sessions, community programs, etc.
